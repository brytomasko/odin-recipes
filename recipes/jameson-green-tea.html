Jameson Green Tea

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • 1 1/2 oz. Jameson
  • 1/2 oz. Peach Schnapps
  • 1/2 oz. Sweet and Sour Mix
  • 1 oz. Green Tea
  • Top with Sprite
  • (optional) Lime Circle

Instructions

  1. Add ice to large glass.
  2. Add the Jameson, Peach Schnapps, Sweet and Sour Mix.
  3. Top with 1 oz. of Green Tea.
  4. Shake and or mix the ingredients
  5. Top off the glass with sprite
  6. Garnish with Lime
  7. Enjoy!
