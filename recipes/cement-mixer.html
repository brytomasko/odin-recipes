Cement Mixer

Description

The cement mixer shot is both one of the most popular shot drinks and, in some opinions, one of the most disgusting. Why it has endured is a great mystery in the bar world. The reason may have a lot to do with the reaction of those who drink it and the laugh their friends get out of watching it go down.

The drink itself does not taste badâ€”citrus and cream are not a poor combination. The real problem is the texture; it is named the cement mixer because it's imagined to be a lot like drinking concrete. If you have a tendency to gag on certain food textures, this shot is probably not for you. It can be a hard drink to get down and there are plenty of tastier shooters to enjoy instead.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz. of Irish Creme Liqueur
  • 1/2 oz. Lime Juice

Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Pour the Irish Creme Liqueur into a large shot glass.
  3. Slowly float the Lime Juice on top.
  4. When you're ready to drink, take the shot and hold it in your mouth.
  5. Shake your head very fast, then swallow.
  6. You just turned your mouth into a cement mixer!
